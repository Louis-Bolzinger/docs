## Downloading the App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Log in to your **Première Copilot Account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download">
    Click the **"Download App"** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select OS">
    Select the correct installer for your operating system:
    - **Windows**
    - **Mac Intel** (Older models)
    - **Mac Silicon** (M1, M2, M3, M4 chips) — *Please ensure you select the correct architecture*.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Installing the Software

<Steps>
  <Step title="Unzip">
    **Unzip** the downloaded file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run Installer">
    **Run the installer**. This will install Première GPT in the correct directory.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart">
    **Restart Adobe Premiere Pro**. It is also recommended, though not mandatory, to restart your computer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  **Security Disclaimer:** You may receive a virus alert from Windows or Mac security. This is a false positive caused by the software being new and not yet fully whitelisted by manufacturers. The software is safe. Please authorize the application in your security settings to proceed.
</Warning>

## Initializing the Extension

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Premiere Pro">
    Open Adobe Premiere Pro.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Extension">
    Go to **Window > Extensions > Première GPT Beta**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In">
    **Log in** using the same credentials created on the website.
  </Step>
  <Step title="First Launch">
    **First Launch:** If this is your first time starting the extension, allow 1–2 minutes for the backend engine to initialize. Subsequent launches will be much faster.
  </Step>
</Steps>

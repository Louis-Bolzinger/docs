## Creating an Account

To begin using Première Copilot, you must first create a user account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the website">
    Go to the [Première Copilot website](https://premierecopilot.com).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the process">
    Click on the **"Download for free"** button located at the top right of the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter details">
    Fill out the form with your first name, last name, and other required details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Click **"Sign up"**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify email">
    **Verify your email:** Go to your inbox and validate your account by clicking the confirmation link.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  **Note:** Upon signing up, you will receive a promo code to purchase the "Podcast" plugin at a discounted rate.
</Note>

## Accessing the Dashboard

Once verified, return to the website and click **"Sign in"** to access your dashboard. From here, you can:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Download App" icon="download">
    Download the latest version of the application.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Book a Demo" icon="calendar">
    Schedule a demo to learn more features.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Join Discord" icon="discord">
    Join our community on Discord.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Affiliate Code" icon="ticket">
    Access your promo code for referrals.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

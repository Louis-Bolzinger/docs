Earn **33% commission** on sales generated through your referrals.

## Eligibility

To become an affiliate, you must:

- Have an active account.
- Have purchased at least one product (Subscription or Podcast plugin).

## How it Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get your code">
    Locate your **custom promo code** in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share">
    Share this code and the link with your contacts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Report sales">
    If a contact makes a purchase using your code (or on your recommendation), send an email to support containing the **email addresses of the contacts who created an account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verification">
    Verification will be performed on our end to confirm the referral.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Payouts

- **Commission Rate:** 33% of the amount paid.
- **Recurring:** Applicable monthly if the user subscribes.
- **Payment Methods:** Payouts are made via PayPal or Stripe refund.

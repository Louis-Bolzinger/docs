Generate short-form content automatically from your long-form videos.

## How to use

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Chat">
    Navigate to the **Chat** interface within the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request Clips">
    Request the AI to find the best moments of the podcast.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Result">
    The tool will analyze the timeline and create **3 different Reels** based on the best highlights.
  </Step>
</Steps>

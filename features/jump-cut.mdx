This tool automatically removes silence from your footage. It is approximately **10x faster** than competitors like AutoPod.

## Usage

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Tool">
    Open the **Tools Menu** (third button, bottom left) and select **Jump Cut**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prepare Sequence">
    Ensure your sequence is open and the playhead is located over an area with both sound and silence.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview">
    Click **"Preview"**. The timeline will highlight areas to keep in **Blue** and areas to remove in **Red**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

![Jump Cut Interface Placeholder](/images/jumpcut.png)

## Parameters

- **Silence Cutoff:** The decibel level considered as silence.
- **Remove Silence Over:** Deletes silence exceeding a specific duration.
- **Keep Segments Over:** Preserves speech segments longer than a specific duration.
- **Padding:** Adds a margin (buffer) before and after cuts to smooth transitions.

## Finalizing

1. Click **"Create Jump Cut"** to execute the cuts.
2. Click **"Delete Disabled Clips"** to remove the cut footage from the timeline.
